Hey, I'm Liv!

I have always been a creative person and definitely found my happy place when I began studying graphic design. One of my favourite quotes reads, "a beautiful design without a great idea won't be remembered, and a great idea without a beautiful design won't be noticed." I love seeing how a beautiful design can really elevate a concept and bring it to the next level. I specialize in branding, photography, and content creation.
